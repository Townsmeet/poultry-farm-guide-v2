### **Are Broiler Eggs Healthy?**
Most chicken eggs are safe for human consumption, except in some cases where the chickens were fed with unhealthy substances. This can be done unknowingly where the chicken feeds on a polluted environment or pasture, while in some cases it is done on purpose in order to achieve a particular goal. like in the case of rapid weight gain.
For example, farmers who raise meat chickens like broilers are always looking for ways to cut costs and maximize profit by feeding their birds what they call “booster”, which in most cases are not organic, but synthetic.
Most of these so-called boosters act by doubling the weight and growth of the broiler within a short period, and if such a bird actually lays eggs, there will be traces of some of these harmful chemicals in the eggs which is obviously unhealthy.
Some other points to note on broiler eggs
-   Research has shown that broiler eggs from synthetically raised broilers can cause cancer, especially in males.
-   A harmful hormone known as Dioxins is found in high quantities in broiler eggs. The [chemical](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chemical_substance) can cause serious health problems like nervous disorders, sometimes miscarriage, etc.
-   There is a huge number of [***unhealthy fats***](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saturated_fat) in broiler eggs that are not good for a healthy individual. If broiler eggs are taken regularly, they can cause many problems like heart problems, obesity, etc.
